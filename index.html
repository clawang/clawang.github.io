About Me

I'm Claire (王宇静), a developer & designer based in SF and NYC. Currently, I work full-time as a front-end engineer at Airbnb and before that I was at Google, working on music at YouTube. I also occasionally do freelance web work for music artists and other clients.

When I'm not doing that, I'm usually traveling, going to live music shows, vintage shopping, cafe-hopping.

I graduated from NYU in 2021 with a B.A. in Computer Science and a minor in Web Programming and Applications.
